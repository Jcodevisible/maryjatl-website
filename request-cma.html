What Is a Comparative Market Analysis

A Comparative Market Analysis (CMA) evaluates your property against recent sales, current listings, and market conditions in your area. It provides a framework for understanding where your home sits in the current market.

A CMA is not an appraisal. It is a professional market assessment that can inform decisions about timing, pricing, and next steps.

When a CMA Is Useful

  • During divorce or separation proceedings requiring property valuation context
  • When court documentation of market value is needed
  • Before making decisions about selling, refinancing, or buying out equity
  • When attorneys or mediators request market data for negotiations

What You Will Receive

Analysis of comparable properties (recent sales and active listings)

Current market condition summary for your area

Estimated market value range based on available data

Documentation formatted for professional or legal use
