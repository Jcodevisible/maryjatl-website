Georgia Buyer Demand Indicators

Key metrics showing current buyer activity in the Georgia real estate market.

Pending Listings

Direct measure of buyer demand. High pending counts mean buyers are actively making offers on homes.

Days on Market

Shows how quickly buyers are purchasing. Fewer days indicates strong demand; more days suggests buyers are hesitant.

Data sourced from Realtor.com via FRED. Updated monthly.
