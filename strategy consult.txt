PAGE TITLE (H1)
---------------
Real Estate Strategy Consult

INTRO COPY
----------
Major life transitions often involve real estate decisions that carry financial, legal, and timing implications.

The Real Estate Strategy Consult is a paid, professional session designed to provide clarity around market context, pricing posture, and next-step options before decisions are made.

This consultation is neutral, documented, and focused on understanding — not pressure.

WHAT THE STRATEGY CONSULT IS
----------------------------
The Strategy Consult is a structured conversation focused on real estate considerations during divorce, separation, or other major life transitions.

It is intended to help you understand:
- Market conditions and timing
- Equity considerations
- Sale-path options
- Documentation that may be required
- Practical next steps

This consult is not a sales meeting and does not require a commitment to list.

WHAT THE STRATEGY CONSULT IS NOT
-------------------------------
The Strategy Consult is not:
- A free discovery call
- A listing presentation
- Legal advice
- Financial planning
- Buyer representation
- Emotional coaching

Guidance is limited to real estate strategy and market context only.

WHAT IS COVERED
---------------
Topics may include:
- Overview of the property and current context
- Market-supported pricing posture
- Timing considerations and potential delay impact
- Sale strategy options (sell now, delay, prepare, court-ordered scenarios)
- Documentation considerations for attorneys or mediation
- Clear explanation of available paths forward

All discussion is handled with neutrality and discretion.

PRICING
-------
Real Estate Strategy Consult: $350

This is a paid professional consultation reflecting the time, analysis, and preparation involved.

If you proceed with a listing, the Strategy Consult fee may be credited at closing.

TIME & FORMAT
-------------
- Duration: Approximately 60 minutes
- Format: Virtual or in-person (as available)
- Preparation: Light pre-session information may be requested to ensure a productive discussion

CONFIDENTIALITY
---------------
All Strategy Consults are handled confidentially.

Information shared during the consult is used solely for the purpose of providing real estate context and guidance.

DISCLAIMERS
-----------
The Strategy Consult does not provide legal or financial advice.

Market observations are based on available data and current conditions and do not guarantee outcomes.
