Divorce & Separation Home Sales

For Attorneys & Judges: Structured listing process with documentation standards appropriate for court proceedings. Timeline coordination with legal milestones. Neutral communication protocols when representing both parties' interests in the sale.

For Homeowners: Guidance through the process of selling a shared property during separation. Clear explanation of market conditions, timing considerations, and what to expect at each stage.

02

Court-Ordered & Time-Sensitive Listings

For Attorneys & Judges: Experience with court-ordered sales and deadline-driven transactions. Documentation of market exposure, pricing adjustments, and offer timelines for legal review.

For Homeowners: When court orders or deadlines require a sale, I help you understand the process, prepare the property appropriately, and navigate timing constraints.

03

Equity & Market Timing Analysis

For Attorneys & Judges: Comparative market analysis with supporting data for equity discussions. Market condition reports that contextualize current value within broader trends.

For Homeowners: Understanding your home's current market position and how timing may affect net proceeds. Data-supported analysis to inform decisions about when and whether to sell.

04

High-Conflict / One-Party Cooperation

For Attorneys & Judges: Protocols for transactions where direct communication between parties is limited or inadvisable. Clear documentation trails and professional boundaries maintained throughout.

For Homeowners: When cooperation is difficult, I provide structured communication and clear processes that allow the sale to proceed professionally regardless of the relationship between parties.
