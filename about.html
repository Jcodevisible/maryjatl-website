Keller Williams Realty Atlanta Partners

I help individuals and families navigate one of life's most significant transitions — selling a home during or after divorce.

Background

I've been a licensed REALTOR in Georgia since 2019, specializing in residential real estate and divorce-related property sales. My approach combines real estate knowledge with an understanding of the financial, legal, and personal complexities that arise during major life transitions.

Before focusing on real estate, I built a career in technology. I currently serve as a Senior Customer Experience Program Manager at Microsoft, where I lead strategic initiatives focused on customer satisfaction and operational processes. With over 15 years of experience in Information Technology — including roles as a Software Engineer, Support Engineer, and Program Manager — I developed analytical and problem-solving skills that inform how I approach real estate transactions.

I hold a Master's degree in Computer Information Systems.

Approach

I work to provide clarity during uncertain times. Before decisions are made, I help clients understand the numbers, the market conditions, and the potential impact of timing.

  • — Neutral representation in emotionally complex situations
  • — Market-supported pricing and timing analysis
  • — Clear documentation for attorneys and decision-makers
  • — Coordination with attorneys, mediators, and other professionals
